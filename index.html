Carl's First Trip to London

He flew out of Toulouse, early in the morning. After arriving in London, he decided he wanted to visit the city.

First he went to the London Bridge

He wanted to take a picture, but didn't have arms, or a camera. So he decided to visit the next spot.

Then to see the Japanese Cherry Blossoms

He had heard they were beautiful, but was sad to see there were no actual cherries.

Afterwards a ride on the London Eye

It was more thrilling than riding the conveyer belt at the airport!

Carl was captured by airport security

Finally, he was tired of the flat beer and chilly temperatures and happy arrive in Florida to join his family.
